Our Story

Ikawa House was founded in 2013 in the heart of Kigali by Amina and David, two Rwandans who believed their country's world-class coffee deserved a world-class home. Rwanda's volcanic highlands — from Huye to Nyamasheke — produce some of the finest washed Arabica beans on the continent, and we've made it our mission to showcase them.

We source directly from smallholder farmers across the Southern and Western Provinces, roast every batch in-house at our Kimihurura roastery, and train our baristas to specialty standard. Ikawa means "coffee" in Kinyarwanda — and for us, it means everything.
